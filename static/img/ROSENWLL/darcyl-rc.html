Darcyl-RC Capsule

Darcyl-RC Capsule



Darcyl- RC is a Nutraceutical supplement designed to support overall joint health and bone strength. With a potent blend of natural ingredients, this supplement aims to enhance joint mobility, reduce inflammation and promote the well-being of your bones and connective tissues.

Ingredients

Rosehip Extract: Packed with antioxidants and anti-inflammatory properties, rosehip extract contributes to joint health by reducing inflammation and supporting tissue repair.

Serrata Extract: Known for its anti-inflammatory effects, Boswellia serrata extract helps alleviate joint pain and discomfort, promoting better mobility.

Collagen Peptide: A key protein for maintaining the integrity of connective tissues, collagen peptide supports joint flexibility and helps in the regeneration of cartilage.

Sodium Hyaluronate: This compound plays a crucial role in lubricating joints and maintaining proper joint function, aiding in reducing friction and discomfort.

Vitamin C: An essential nutrient for collagen synthesis, vitamin C contributes to the health of bones and cartilage, promoting overall joint strength.

Vitamin D3: Vitamin D3: Vital for calcium absorption and bone health, vitamin D3 supports the maintenance of strong and healthy bones.



Therapy Area:

Darcyl- RC is formulated to address concerns related to joint health and bone strength. It is particularly beneficial for individuals experiencing joint discomfort, stiffness or looking to support and maintain optimal bone health.


Form:

Darcyl- RC is available in Capsule form, making it easy to incorporate into your daily routine. The capsules are designed for easy digestion and absorption, ensuring that you receive the maximum benefits of the carefully selected ingredients.


Benefits:

Joint Mobility: The combination of rosehip extract, Boswellia serrata extract, and collagen peptide works synergistically to enhance joint flexibility and mobility.

Inflammation Reduction:The anti-inflammatory properties of the ingredients help in reducing inflammation, easing joint pain, and improving overall joint comfort.

Connective Tissue Support: Collagen peptide contributes to the health of connective tissues, promoting the regeneration of cartilage and maintaining joint integrity.

Bone Strength: The inclusion of vitamin C and vitamin D3 supports bone health, aiding in the absorption of essential minerals and ensuring the maintenance of strong and healthy bones.

Packing: 1X10X10 hard gelatine capsules blister/strip packing in box.

Dosage: As directed by the Health Professional. Capsule should be swallowed whole. It should not be opened, chewed or crushed.
