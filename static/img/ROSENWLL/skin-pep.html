Skin PEP



SKINPEP is a comprehensive skincare supplement formulated with a blend of potent ingredients to nourish and support skin health. Available in a convenient packing, is carefully crafted to provide a holistic approach to skincare. With a combination of Marine Collagen Peptides, Reduced Glutathione, Hyaluronic Acid, and a powerhouse of vitamins including E (50%), A, C, D3, Folic Acid, Niacinamide, Pantothenic Acid, and Choline, SKINPEP aims to promote skin vitality and radiance from within.

Ingredients

Marine Collagen Peptides: Supports skin elasticity and hydration.

Reduced Glutathione: An antioxidant for cellular protection.

Hyaluronic Acid: Helps maintain skin moisture and suppleness.

Vitamin E (50%): A powerful antioxidant for skin health.

Vitamin A: Essential for skin repair and regeneration.

Vitamin C: Promotes collagen synthesis and skin brightness.

Vitamin D3: Supports overall skin health.

Folic Acid: Contributes to cell growth and regeneration

Niacinamide: Supports even skin tone and texture

Pantothenic Acid: Aids in skin hydration.

Choline:Essential for cell membrane integrity.



Therapy Area:

SKINPEP is designed to address various aspects of skin health, including:

Anti-Aging: Collagen, antioxidants, and vitamins contribute to skin elasticity and protection against premature aging.

Hydration: \Ingredients like Hyaluronic Acid and Pantothenic Acid support skin moisture.

Even Skin Tone: Niacinamide helps maintain an even complexion.

Cell Regeneration: Vitamins A and D3, along with Folic Acid, contribute to cell growth and regeneration.


Form:

SKINPEP comes in individual 7.50g sachets, making it convenient for daily use. The box packing of 1x10 ensures an ample supply for an extended skincare regimen.


Benefits:

Collagen Support: Marine Collagen Peptides contribute to skin elasticity and hydration.

Antioxidant Defense: Reduced Glutathione, Vitamin E, and Vitamin C provide robust antioxidant protection.

Hydration Boost: Hyaluronic Acid and Pantothenic Acid help maintain skin moisture.

Vitamin Powerhouse: A combination of essential vitamins supports overall skin health and radiance.

Cell Regeneration: Folic Acid, Vitamins A, and D3 contribute to cell growth and regeneration.

Even Skin Tone: Niacinamide supports a balanced and even skin complexion.

Packing:(1X10) Sachets box of 7.50 gm. each

Dosage:One sachet daily or as directed by dietician/health practitioner.
