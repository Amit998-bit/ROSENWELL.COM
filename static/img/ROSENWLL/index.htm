About Us

Welcome to Rosenwell Healthcare Pvt. Ltd.

Established in 2002 as Nutrex Healthcare, our journey has been one of continuous growth and commitment to healthcare excellence. In 2017, we underwent a significant transformation, rebranding ourselves as Rosenwell Healthcare Pvt Ltd. This pivotal moment marked a new chapter in our mission to elevate healthcare standards globally. As a dynamic pharmaceutical manufacturing and marketing company, Rosenwell Healthcare operates on the principles of innovation, integrity, and inclusivity. Since our inception, we have been driven by a relentless pursuit of excellence in both the production and promotion of pharmaceutical products. With a focus on research and development, we strive to bring forth cutting-edge solutions that address the evolving healthcare needs of our consumers.
