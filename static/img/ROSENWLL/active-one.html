Active One TM



ACTIVE ONETM is a comprehensive nutritional supplement designed to support overall vitality and well-being. Formulated with a blend of essential vitamins, minerals, amino acids and herbal extracts. ACTIVE ONETM provides a synergistic combination of nutrients to enhance energy levels, mental focus, and immune function. Each sachet of ACTIVE ONETM contains a precise formulation of ingredients, carefully selected to promote optimal health and performance.

Ingredients

L-Taurine: Supports cardiovascular health and boosts energy levels.

L-Carnitine: Aids in fat metabolism and energy production.

L-Glutamine: Supports muscle recovery and immune function.

Ginseng Extract (10%): Adaptogenic herb that helps the body cope with stress and fatigue.

Caffeine: Enhances alertness and mental focus.

Inositol: Supports nerve function and mood balance.

Lycopene (10%): Antioxidant that may protect against oxidative damage.

Niacinamide: Vitamin B3 derivative involved in energy metabolism and skin health.

Zinc Sulphate: Essential mineral for immune function and wound healing.

Vitamin C: Antioxidant crucial for collagen synthesis and immune health.

Vitamin B6: Supports energy metabolism and neurotransmitter synthesis.

Folic Acid: Vitamin B9 essential for cell division and DNA synthesis.

Cyanocobalamin: Vitamin B12 crucial for red blood cell formation and neurological function.

Potassium Chloride: Electrolyte important for nerve and muscle function.

Sodium Chloride: Electrolyte necessary for maintaining fluid balance and nerve function.
