Colla- T2



Colla-T2 is an advanced dietary supplement meticulously formulated to provide targeted support for joint health and mobility. Featuring a potent blend of key ingredients, including Collagen Peptide Type 2, Chondroitin Sulphate, Rosehip Extract, and Vitamin D3, Colla-T2 is designed to nourish and maintain the health of joints, cartilage, and connective tissues.

Ingredients

Collagen Peptide Type 2 (40mg): A specific type of collagen known for its role in supporting joint structure and function, Collagen Peptide Type 2 helps maintain the integrity of cartilage.

Chondroitin Sulphate (200mg): This natural compound is essential for the formation of cartilage and helps in maintaining joint flexibility and resilience.

Rosehip Extract (200mg): Rich in antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds, Rosehip Extract contributes to joint health by reducing inflammation and supporting the repair of connective tissues.

Vitamin D3 (400IU): Essential for calcium absorption and bone health, Vitamin D3 plays a key role in supporting overall joint and bone integrity.



Therapy Area:

Colla-T2 is specifically formulated to address the needs of individuals seeking comprehensive joint support. It is suitable for those experiencing joint discomfort, stiffness, or individuals looking to proactively promote joint health and mobility.


Form:

Colla-T2 is presented in non-vegetarian hard gelatin capsules, ensuring easy digestion and optimal absorption of the carefully selected ingredients. The convenient capsule form allows for simple integration into your daily routine.


Benefits:

Joint Health Support: Collagen Peptide Type 2 and Chondroitin Sulphate work together to support the health and flexibility of joints, promoting overall mobility.

Cartilage Integrity : Collagen Peptide Type 2 contributes to the maintenance of cartilage integrity, aiding in the cushioning and protection of joints.

Anti-Inflammatory Properties: Rosehip Extract, with its anti-inflammatory compounds, helps reduce joint inflammation, easing discomfort and supporting joint function.

Bone Strength: The inclusion of Vitamin D3 supports bone health, contributing to the maintenance of strong and healthy bones.

Packing: 3x10 veg capsules in Alu/Alu packing in box.

Dosage: 1-2 Capsules daily after meal or as Directed by Health Professionals.
