Wellcumin



Wellcumin is a cutting-edge dietary supplement that combines the powerful benefits of nano-curcumin, lycopene, and piperine in a convenient vegetarian capsule. This unique formulation is designed to provide advanced antioxidant support, promote overall well-being, and enhance the bioavailability of curcumin for maximum effectiveness.

Ingredients

Nano-Curcumin (10%) - 500mg:Known for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, nano-curcumin is a highly bioavailable form of curcumin that supports overall health and helps combat oxidative stress.

Lycopene (10%) - 25mg: A potent antioxidant found in tomatoes, lycopene contributes to the neutralization of free radicals, supporting cardiovascular health and promoting skin and prostate well-being.

Piperine (95%) - 5mg: Derived from black pepper, piperine enhances the bioavailability of curcumin, maximizing its absorption and efficacy in the body.



Therapy Area:

Wellcumin is formulated to address a wide range of health concerns, making it suitable for individuals seeking antioxidant support, anti-inflammatory benefits, and overall well-being. It may be particularly beneficial for those looking to support joint health, cardiovascular function, and immune system resilience.


Form:

Wellcumin is presented in vegetarian capsules, providing a convenient and easily digestible form for daily supplementation. The use of vegetarian capsules ensures that individuals with various dietary preferences can incorporate this advanced formula into their wellness routine.


Benefits:

Advanced Antioxidant Support: The combination of nano-curcumin and lycopene provides powerful antioxidant support, helping to neutralize free radicals and protect cells from oxidative stress.

Anti-Inflammatory Properties: Nano-curcumin is known for its anti-inflammatory effects, which may help alleviate inflammation and support joint health.

Cardiovascular Health: Lycopene has been associated with cardiovascular benefits, promoting heart health and maintaining healthy cholesterol levels.

Enhanced Bioavailability: Piperine plays a crucial role in enhancing the bioavailability of curcumin, ensuring that the body can absorb and utilize this powerful antioxidant more effectively.

Overall Well-Being: Glycolift assists in toning and firming the skin, enhancing its overall resilience and smoothness.

Packing: 3x10 HPMC veg capsules in Alu/Alu packing in box

Dosage:1-2 Capsules daily after meal or as Directed by Health Professionals.
