Osmolin



Osmolin is an advanced anti-stretch mark cream specially formulated to address skin elasticity and hydration concerns. Enriched with a synergistic blend of key ingredients, including Osmogeline, Phyto Bioactive, Aquaxyl, and Glycolift, Osmolin is designed to nourish and support the skin, helping to reduce the appearance of stretch marks and promote overall skin health.

Ingredients

Osmogeline (2%): A unique compound known for its moisturizing properties, helping to improve skin elasticity and suppleness.

Phyto Bioactive (2%): Derived from plant sources, Phyto Bioactive contributes to the overall health of the skin, promoting natural rejuvenation.

Folic Acid and Zinc: Vital for neural tube development and immune system support.

Aquaxyl (1%): A hydration-boosting ingredient that helps optimize the skin's water reserves, enhancing moisturization and reducing dryness.

Glycolift (2%): Known for its toning and firming effects, Glycolift contributes to skin smoothness and resilience.



Therapy Area:

Osmolin is specifically formulated for individuals seeking to address and prevent stretch marks, especially during periods of rapid skin expansion such as pregnancy or significant weight fluctuations. It can be used on various areas of the body prone to stretch marks, promoting skin elasticity and reducing the visibility of existing marks.

Intensive Hydration: The inclusion of Aquaxyl helps optimize skin hydration, reducing dryness and promoting a smoother texture.
